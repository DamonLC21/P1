About

From Charleston, SC, I am an all around creative with a passion for Arts and Design. As an illustrator I taught myself how to paint at 16 years old bringing to life many abstract-realism contemporary acrylic paintings currently in the homes of various art collectors . Honing in on my ability to learn new things quickly I taught myself Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, and many more design softwares as well as Adobe Audition which is an audio engineering DAW. With interest in applying my creativity and design skills to the workforce I stumbled upon Web Development which has really been a game changer. My focus on having an awesome career where I can create as well as challenge myself mentally everyday lies here in Web Development. I aspire to create multiple projects that will reach the hands of millions one day. I invite you all on my journey and hopefully a pair of those hands will be yours!
